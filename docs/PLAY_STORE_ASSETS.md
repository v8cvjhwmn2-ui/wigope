## Full Description

Wigope Pay helps you manage everyday payments with a fast recharge-first experience.

Use Wigope Pay for mobile recharges, DTH, FASTag, electricity and key bill payment services. Add money to your secure wallet, track transaction history, view cashback and referral rewards, and discover gift cards and vouchers powered by Hubble staging catalogue during launch testing.

Built by Wigope Technologies Pvt Ltd for a simple, secure and made-for-India payment experience.

Key features:
- Mobile recharge, DTH recharge, FASTag and electricity payments
- Secure Wigope wallet top-up
- Gift cards and voucher deals
- Referral rewards and reward chain
- Transaction histories with status tracking
- Hindi and English app copy
- Secure OTP login

## Data Safety Notes

- Mobile number is used for OTP login and account identity.
- PAN/Aadhaar data is collected only for KYC/DMT flows and encrypted at rest.
- Payment data is processed through payment partners; card/UPI credentials are not stored by Wigope.
- App tokens are stored in Android encrypted storage.
